Resume

I want people to have fun and enjoy themselves. Developing games is a way for me to do that.

My name is Frank Alfano and I am a game developer! I have been programming since I was in 5th grade, and have mainly worked with Unity and C#. I find programming video games to be very relaxing and rewarding, so most of my free time is spent learning and working on personal projects. When I'm not developing, I am riding my bike or just enjoying the outdoors.

Blockchain

itch.io
Github

This game was made in 48 hours for the GameMaker's Toolkit Game Jam 2021! The theme for the jam was "Chained Together", and so this game is all about chaining various blocks together to solve puzzles. The game was made with Unity and C#, with art done in paint.NET. I did all of the programming, art, and helped with the level design. My friend Joe Ferrugio helped with the level design and made the music. Overall, we placed in the top 600 of submissions, out of 6000 total.

Collab

Github

Collab is a game I made by myself in the span of about 2 months. It's a 2 player puzzle platforming game where both players need to work together to solve puzzles. It is the first "complete" game that I have made, or at least one with more than 30 minutes of gameplay and a good deal of polish. Even though this game isn't that special on the outside, it still holds a nice place in my mind.

I used Unity and C# to make this game, along with paint.NET for the art. I recommend checking out the Github page if you are interested in learning about the mechanics!
