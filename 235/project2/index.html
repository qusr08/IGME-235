Description / How To Play

In this game, you will be given 1 minute to answer as many questions as you can. The category of the questions is just general trivia so be prepared for anything.

If you get one right, you gain 5 seconds back to your timer, plus some points. If you get one wrong, you lost 5 seconds and gain no points. The faster you answer a question, the more points you get!

The goal is to get as many points as possible!
