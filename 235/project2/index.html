Description / How To Play

In this game, you will be given 1 minute to answer as many questions as you can.

You can select which trivia category you want below. Choose "General Knowledge" for a variety of topics. You can also select the difficulty of the questions.

If you get a question right, you gain 5 seconds back to your timer, plus some points. If you get a question wrong, you lost 5 seconds and gain no points. The faster you answer a question, the more points you get! You gain more points per question depending on how high you set the difficulty as well. When you run out of time, the game is over!

The goal is to get as many points as possible!
